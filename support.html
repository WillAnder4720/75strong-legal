Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my challenge?

Tap the circular arrow icon in the top-left corner of the Today tab. You'll be taken through the setup flow to choose your current day and start fresh.

My progress photos aren't showing up.

Make sure 75 Strong has permission to access your camera and photo library. Go to iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos → 75 Strong and set it to "All Photos" or "Selected Photos."

The AI Coach isn't responding.

Check your internet connection. Free users are limited to 10 messages per day — if you've hit the limit it resets at midnight. Upgrade to 75 Strong Pro for unlimited messages.

Journey Analysis or Physique Scan is locked.

Free users get one lifetime trial of each feature. After using your trial, upgrade to 75 Strong Pro for unlimited access.

Apple Health data isn't appearing in Analytics.

Go to iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → 75 Strong and ensure Steps, Heart Rate, and Active Energy are turned on. Then re-open the Analytics tab.

How do I cancel my Pro subscription?

Open iPhone Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → 75 Strong → Cancel Subscription. You'll keep Pro access until the end of the current billing period.

I bought Pro but it's showing as free.

Open the app, go to Settings, and tap "Manage" next to 75 Strong Pro. If the issue persists, tap "Restore Purchases" on the Pro upgrade screen.

I accidentally deleted the app and lost my data.

75 Strong stores data locally on your device. Deleting the app permanently removes your challenge history. We recommend using iCloud Backup to protect your device data. Unfortunately we cannot recover deleted app data.

Subscription & Billing

75 Strong Pro is an optional auto-renewable subscription available in two plans:

  • Monthly — $1.99/month
  • Yearly — $14.99/year (save 37%)

Payment is charged to your Apple ID. To manage or cancel, go to iPhone Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions.

For billing disputes or refund requests, please contact Apple directly as all payments are processed by Apple.
