Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my challenge?

Go to Settings → Profile → scroll down and tap "Reset Challenge". You can choose which day to restart from.

How do I enable notifications?

Go to Settings → Notifications. Enable notifications and customize which tasks you want reminders for. You can also schedule specific times for each task.

How do I sync my data across devices?

Make sure you're logged in with the same account on all devices. Your data automatically syncs when you're connected to the internet.

How do I connect Apple Health?

When you first open the app, you'll be prompted to connect Apple Health. You can also enable it later in iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → 75 Strong.

What features are included in Premium?

Premium includes:

  • AI-Powered Photo Analysis
  • Personal AI Coach
  • Advanced Analytics with Health Insights
  • Detailed Charts and Trend Analysis
How do I cancel my subscription?

Open iOS Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions → 75 Strong → Cancel Subscription. Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Profile → Log Out → Delete Account. This will permanently delete all your data.

About 75 Hard

The 75 Hard challenge is a 75-day mental toughness program that requires completing these tasks every day:

  • Two 45-minute workouts (one must be outdoors)
  • Follow a diet (no cheat meals or alcohol)
  • Drink 1 gallon of water
  • Read 10 pages of non-fiction
  • Take a progress photo

Missing any task means starting over at Day 1. 75 Strong helps you track and complete this challenge.
