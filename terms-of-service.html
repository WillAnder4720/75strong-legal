75 Strong is a fitness tracking mobile application designed to help users complete the 75 Hard challenge, a 75-day mental toughness program. The App provides:

  • Workout & Diet Logging: Record workout details and diet information

  • Weight Tracking: Log and monitor weight changes
  • Challenge History: View your complete 75-day journey
