2. Description of the App

75 Strong is a personal fitness challenge tracker designed to help you complete a structured 75-day program. Features include:

  • Daily task tracking (strength training, cardio, diet, water intake, reading, progress photos)
  • AI Coach — a conversational assistant powered by OpenAI's API
  • Journey Analysis — AI-powered review of your logged notes
  • Physique Progression Scan — AI comparison of two progress photos
  • Apple Health integration for steps, heart rate, and active energy data
  • Weight tracking and analytics dashboard
